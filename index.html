Process & Approach

How I Design

Every project follows a deliberate process — from understanding the problem to delivering a polished, user-tested solution. Here's how I work.

01 — Problem
Understand the Challenge

I start by deeply understanding who the users are and what friction they face. User research, competitive analysis, and stakeholder interviews guide this phase.

02 — Solution
Ideate & Design

Wireframes evolve into high-fidelity prototypes in Figma. I explore multiple directions before converging on the most effective and elegant solution.

03 — Tools Used
Figma, Vibe Coding, Research

Figma for design, prototyping, and collaboration. Vibe coding to bring concepts to life. Written research and content to communicate ideas clearly.

04 — Outcome
Deliver & Iterate

Final deliverables include polished screens, design system components, and handoff-ready specs. I stay involved through implementation to ensure the vision is preserved.
